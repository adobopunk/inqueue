Built By The Community. Fueled By Passion.

In Queue was founded by esports veterans who grew tired of seeing competition take a backseat to commercial interests. We’ve worked across every level of the industry – from running local LANs to producing global championships – and we’re channeling that experience into building something different.

We’re not trying to reinvent esports – we’re fighting to preserve what made it great while pushing it forward. This isn’t about “disrupting” esports. It’s about returning it to the people who made it matter: you.

Who We Are

We’re the disillusioned architects of esports’ biggest stages – now building something that we're deeply passionate about.

Directing all things operational is Cory Sobon, a veteran tournament director who's administered over 300 events and paid out more than $3 million in prizes. Having worked with giants like Activision, Respawn, and Xbox while founding the community-focused Proving Grounds circuit, Cory, known in the gaming sphere as Mazida, brings both elite experience and a refusal to compromise competitive integrity.

Our creative vision comes from Tiny Pond - the powerhouse creative studio run by partners Mateo Julia-Wise and Tifa Jade (TJ). Mateo brings six years of esports design mastery, from grassroots tournaments to events for Tier 2 Overwatch, Fortnite, and Rocket League.

TJ merges UFC production pedigree with deep esports roots that date back to the H1Z1 Pro League. She's directed cinematic event visuals for some of the biggest tournaments and influencers in the gaming sphere. Together, their work has ignited hype for over 400 tournaments, commanding main stages with 200K+ live viewers and amassing 100M+ impressions.

We're proud to be employee-owned and fiercely independent. Every dollar we earn goes back into the community – funding better tournaments, fairer opportunities, and the authentic esports experience fans deserve. No outside investors dictating our direction. No profit-above-all mentality. Just the pure competitive spirit that started it all.

Why We're Here

Our mission is simple: to create premiere competitive experiences that stay true to esports’ roots. We believe world-class tournaments don’t require compromising authenticity. Through open formats, community-driven decisions, and production that prioritizes the essence of competition, we’re here to prove esports can evolve without losing its soul.

This is esports on its own terms. Welcome to the Queue. Are you next?
