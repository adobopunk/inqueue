A one of a kind Valorant tournament experience. Featured in Riot Games' official Valorant Champions Tour, Valorant Select featured character bans and selections as part of its tournament format. Tiny Pond artists designed a custom broadcast experience for this event, allowing viewers to watch map picks/bans, and character selections play out in real time through custom API integration.
