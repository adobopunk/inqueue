Fortnite Reunion

Client: Epic Games

Scope: Branding, Video Trailer, Broadcast Design

Turnaround: 3 Weeks

Designer: Mateo Julia-Wise
Animator: Tifa Jade

When Epic Games announced the return of the battle royale map that started it all, the Fortnite community was stoked. Our artists had the exciting opportunity to develop the branding for Fortnite Reunion, a $100,000 tournament featuring some of the title's biggest creators.

1

The animation concept we started with was a cake that looked like the map that was making its big return. Because the event was called Reunion, we wanted to create a sense of celebration, and what better way to celebrate any reunion than with a cake?
