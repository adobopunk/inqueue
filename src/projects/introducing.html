Today marks the official launch of In Queue, an independent esports production company built by veterans to put competitive integrity and community back at the heart of gaming.

We're tearing up the traditional playbook to create tournaments where players set the rules, skill determines the spotlight, and every match gets the production quality it deserves.

The Team Behind the Vision

Leading the charge is Operations Director Cory "Mazida" Sobon, whose 4+ years at BoomTV saw him administer over 300 tournaments and distribute $3 million in prizes through partnerships with Activision, Respawn, and Xbox.

He's joined by creative visionaries Mateo Julia-Wise and Tifa Jade (TJ), whose combined expertise has shaped 400+ tournaments reaching 200K+ concurrent viewers and generating 100M+ social impressions for titles like Fortnite, Halo, and Valorant.

In Queue is our chance to prove tournaments can thrive when you cut out the middlemen and listen to the people who actually compete.

Our Foundational Pillars

In Queue has codified its philosophy into non-negotiable operating principles:

Integrity

Staying true to the soul of competition. Radical transparency with the community. Success is measured in the unforgettable moments we help create, not profit margins.

Community

Tournaments are shaped by the community. The input of players and fans is our most valuable asset. We're passionate about shaping a space all about mutual respect and a love of the game.

Opportunity

An esports ecosystem where background, connections, and geography don't dictate opportunity. Greatness can come from anywhere. We'll uplift stories of the most skilled players, regardless of follower count. Bring your best and we'll handle the rest.

Excellence

We don’t do ‘good enough.’ Neither should you. We promise to treat every detail like it’s the grand finals. Premium experiences, no compromises. Push greatness. If you talk trash—back it up. Respect the craft, respect each other.

These are the foundations that shape how we run In Queue, as well as our expectations from the community when contributing, competing, and engaging within our spaces. You can read more about our commitment to these values here.

First on the Docket

In Queue has already begun laying the groundwork for its first wave of events, with announcements coming soon. Players and fans can expect to hear about our first Apex Legends event in the coming week.

Apex Legends Ladies Night events remain a high priority for us as they did under Proving Grounds, but finding the correct brand to back the program is important to us. We're working hard to find a brand with values that align with our goals, and hope to share news with you soon.

Previous Proving Grounds fans and players can look forward to a revamped Discord launch this Friday, with an exciting set of new features. Proving Grounds veterans, your new home is around the corner. If you haven't already joined the Discord, this is a great time to dive in.
