A Small Team with Huge Creative Potential.

Cory Sobon, Tifa Jade, and Mateo Julia-Wise collaborated for nearly three years at BoomTV, working with top brands and industry leaders to bring competitive and entertaining online experiences to life.

Now, we're excited to bring that expertise to Tiny Pond and offer it to you directly.

At Tiny Pond, we operate as a cooperative, focused on collaboration and mutual support. Without a traditional hierarchy of managers, CEOs, or shareholders to answer to, our goal is simple: produce great results. That means every investment in Tiny Pond directly supports our cause: making more awesome stuff, for you!

Let’s explore how Tiny Pond can bring your ideas to life. We can't wait to work with you.

Our Values

We're big on values at Tiny Pond. When you work with us, you know what we stand for: the pillars that guide both our work, and our conduct as a team.

Embrace Collaboration. Like frogs in a pond, we know the best results come when we all leap together. Fluidly working together keeps our ecosystem healthy.

Own Your Work. We take pride in our leaps — and we’re always ready to explain our thinking process. Accountability means standing by our work and being transparent about our decisions.

Happy People, Happy Frogs. Here, humans make all the art, not AI. Likewise, we're invested in our clients as people, not just business partners. We're all about human connections and healthy relationships.

Trust in the Pond. Our goal is to keep things clear and open — no murky waters here. Trust goes both ways! We remain clear and open through our whole collaboration, and we expect you to grant us the trust we need to make the creative decisions you count on.

Remote-First, Remote-Always. With team members across the US, we use technology to keep us all on the same lily pad, making sure distance doesn’t slow us down.

Continuous Evolution. Just like a tadpole transforms into a frog, we’re all about growth and change. We embrace every opportunity to evolve, learn, and improve, ensuring we stay fresh, innovative, and ready to take on any challenge.

Cultivate Long-Term Partnerships. We don't want you to just dip your toes in the pond. Our aim is to build long-lasting relationships that grow over time, just like a pond’s ecosystem. Together, we’ll develop a deep understanding based on trust and open, friendly collaboration.

Do Good, Be Good. We prioritize relationships that share our commitment to making the world a better place. We're pro sustainable practices and positive social impact initiatives. A thriving pond benefits every frog, fish, and friend within it.

Excellence in Everything We Do. We’re dedicated to hopping above and beyond. Our goal is to deliver work that exceeds expectations. When you our team loose, expect us to naturally become your go-to creative solution.
