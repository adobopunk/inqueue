The three of us—Cory Sobon, Tifa Jade, and Mateo Julia-Wise—collaborated for nearly three years at BoomTV, where we brought exceptional gaming experiences to life through dynamic branding, engaging videos, and a strong commitment to professionalism and care in our work.

Now, we're excited to bring that expertise to Tiny Pond and offer it to you directly.

At Tiny Pond, we operate as a cooperative, focused on collaboration and mutual support. Without a traditional hierarchy of managers or a CEO to answer to, our goal is simple: produce great results. That means every investment in Tiny Pond directly supports our cause: making more awesome stuff, for you!

Let’s explore how Tiny Pond can bring your ideas to life. We can't wait to work with you.

Our Values

We believe that the best results come when we all swim together. We’re committed to open communication and shared problem-solving, ensuring that the best ideas surface naturally. Through our collaborative approach, we harness our collective strengths and expertise to create impactful results.

Embracing underserved perspectives enriches our work and deepens its impact. Our team is committed to partnering with and supporting causes that make a positive difference. On this team, we foster an environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered — like every drop that contributes to the vibrant ecosystem we create together.

We take pride in our leaps — and we’re always ready to explain our thinking process. Accountability means standing by our work and being transparent about our decisions, fostering trust and integrity in everything we do.

At Tiny Pond, we believe the best results come from expertise and experience. We craft our art with human hands, not AI. We invest in our clients as people, not just business partners. We're most successful when we're celebrating human connections and nurturing healthy relationships.

Our goal is to keep things clear and open — no murky waters here. Trust goes both ways! We maintain transparency throughout our collaboration and ask that you grant us the trust we need to make the creative decisions you rely on. Together, we can navigate the waters with clarity and purpose.

Just like a tadpole transforms into a frog, we’re dedicated to growth and change, ensuring we remain fresh, innovative, and ready to tackle any challenge. With a strong commitment to innovation, alongside a team who embraces research and development, we'll never back down from a worthy challenge.

We don't want you to just dip your toes in the pond. Our aim is to build long-lasting relationships that grow over time, just like a pond’s ecosystem. Together, we’ll develop a valuable partnership based on trust and open, friendly collaboration.

We prioritize relationships that align with our commitment to making the world better for everyone. We advocate for sustainable practices and positive social impact initiatives, understanding that a thriving pond benefits all. Together, we will work to create a more sustainable and vibrant future.

We’re dedicated to hopping above and beyond. Our goal is to deliver work that exceeds expectations. When you collaborate with our team, you can expect us to become your go-to creative solution, naturally adapting to meet your needs and bringing all your visions to life.
